## Superstore Sales & Profit Analysis

### Project Overview

This project analyzes Superstore sales data to understand how the business is performing.
The main goal is to find sales trends, profit issues, customer behavior, and business insights that can help improve decision-making.

The analysis answers real business questions given by the client and turns raw data into clear insights and recommendations.

### Dataset Used

* Superstore Dataset (Excel)

Contains information about:

* Orders
* Sales
* Profit
* Products & Categories
* Regions & States
* Customers & Segments
* Discounts
* Order dates (time data)

### Project Objectives

This project focuses on answering the following key business questions:

1. Overall Sales & Profit Summary
    * Total number of orders
    * Total sales revenue
    * Total profit
    * Average order value
    * Average profit per order

2. Product & Category Performance
* Which categories generate the highest sales
* Which categories generate the highest profit
* Identify low-profit or loss-making products

3. Time-Based Trends & Seasonality
* Monthly, quarterly, and yearly sales trends
* Identify busy months and slow months
* Understand seasonal peaks and dips

4. Region & Location Analysis
* Which regions and states perform best
* Identify underperforming regions
* Find areas that need better marketing or strategy

5. Customer Segmentation & Behavior
* Identify high-value and loyal customers
* Analyze which customer segment brings:
    * Most sales
    * Most profit
* Identify least profitable customer segments

6. Discount & Profitability Analysis
* Check how discounts impact profit
* Identify discount levels that cause losses
* Suggest better discount strategies

7. Actionable Business Recommendations
Based on the analysis:
* Which products to promote or stop selling
* Best time to run marketing campaigns
* How to target top customers and regions
* Inventory planning suggestions

---

### Key Findings (Short Summary)

* Technology category gives the highest profit
* Furniture has high sales but very low profit
* Tables and Bookcases are loss-making products
* Q4 (especially November) is the busiest season
* West and East regions perform best
* Central region needs improvement
* High discounts (30% or more) cause losses
* Low discounts (0-20%) are profitable
